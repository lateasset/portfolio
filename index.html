3D-RELATED UNIVERSITY PROJECTS

Master's Thesis at Politecnico di Torino

Title: "Holographic Augment Reality device tracking"

Subtitle: "Tracking algorithms design and development for Microsoft Hololens"

As the name implies, the project revolved around the development of an AR solution for position tracking of objects as captured by a Hololens 1 device; it was developed as a client inside Unity engine with accessory networking elements and UWP implementation, and stand-alone server leveraging the OpenCV library in C++.

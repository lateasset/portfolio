GAMES & 3D-RELATED UNIVERSITY PROJECTS

(2023) Master's Thesis at Politecnico di Torino

Title: "Holographic Augment Reality device tracking"

Subtitle: "Tracking algorithms design and development for Microsoft Hololens"

The project revolved around the development of an AR solution for position tracking of objects as captured by a Hololens 1 device; it was developed as a client inside Unity engine with accessory networking elements and UWP implementation, along with a stand-alone server leveraging the OpenCV library in C++.

(2018-2020) The last chance

Multi-part project that encompassed two courses (3D basics and animation), the subject are WWII tanks in the early eastern front battles.

The assets were modelled in Blender, shaded and rigged to simulate track/wheel dynamics; they were first rendered inside scenes making use of simple physics simulation and lighting, next I built up on all of that and expanded to more comprehensive volumetrics/particle systems, soft bodies and human characters, while also being required to edit a video. An OpenGL game (now legacy, at best) was also produced, to learn more see PERSONAL GAME PROJECTS -> The last chanche: Remake.
